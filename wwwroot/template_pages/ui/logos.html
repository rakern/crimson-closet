UA Logos

Official University of Alabama logos and identifiers are included in the template for usage in the header. If your departmental identifier is not listed here, please contact us.

To change the logo in the header to match your college, division or department, you will need to change the class name in the header. Locate the ua-logo class name in the header. By default, it uses the standard ua-logo-stacked class to display the primary UA wordmark. You will want to change this to match your organization.

If you are the School of Law, for example, you will locate your logo in the table below and change the ua-logo-stacked class to ua-logo-school-law.

Note: The square "Capstone A" logo is not included in the below examples. However, it should always be included when used in combination with your identifier. Please refer to the University of Alabama's copyright policy.
